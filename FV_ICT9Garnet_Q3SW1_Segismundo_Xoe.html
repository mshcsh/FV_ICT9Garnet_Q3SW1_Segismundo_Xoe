The Disastrous Life of Saiki K

The Disastrous Life of Saiki K is a Japanese manga illustrated by Shūichi Asō

SYNOPSIS

Possessing psychic abilities may appear advantageous to the average person; However, this is not the case at all for Kusuo Saiki. He believes that this so-called blessing is nothing more than a curse because he is endowed with a wide range of supernatural abilities, including telepathy and x-ray vision. Kusuo's only goal is a normal, hassle-free life in which ignorance is bliss, despite the constant inconveniences caused by his powers. Sadly, a psychic's life is far from tranquil. Even though Kusuo tries to keep his powers a secret from his classmates, he accidentally attracts the attention of a lot of strange people, like the empty-headed Riki Nendou and the liar Shun Kaidou. Kusuo discovers, as he is forced to deal with the craziness of those around him, that the normal life he has been striving for is much more difficult to achieve than he anticipated.

Characters:

Kusuo Saiki

Kusuo Saiki is the main protagonist of The Disastrous Life of Saiki K. This high school student has all kinds of psychic abilities (telepathy, superhuman strength, teleportation, etc). He has green glasses, and two pink antennae (which help him contain his powers). His main goal is to survive school with getting as little attention as possible.

Mikoto Aiura

Mikoto Aiura is an oracle or a soothsayer. She has the fashion sense of a gyaru. As opposed to Kusuo, she openly shows her powers. She uses her powers for good and helps anyone who is in need.

Kaidou Shun

Shun Kaidou is a self-proclaimed member of "The Jet Black Wings". He thinks that the world is controlled by an evil organization called The Dark Reunion. He is often cowardly but will endanger himself for the people he cares about.

Riki Nendou

Riki Nendou is an obedient and dull-witted kid. He enjoys helping individuals and focusing on the powerless. Riki's prevailing characteristics are his clear absence of knowledge and the scar to his left side eye. As a result of his looks, he is in some cases deciphered as a trouble maker.

Teruhashi Kokomi

Kokomi Teruhashi is one of the primary characters of the series. At the point when Kokomi was first presented, she declares herself as gorgeous and decent, the encapsulation of an ideal, beautiful young lady. Despite the fact that her appearance is appealing, her graciousness is an act. She is a trickster, making her ready to control and convince almost everybody with her excellence and effectively moves away from others with basic reasons. As the most well known young lady in school, she is profoundly friendly.
